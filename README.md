## Coordinate board
The program takes (x, y) coordinates coresponding to the grid below:
```
- - - - - - - - - - -
(0,0) | (1,0) | (2,0)
- - - - - - - - - - -
(0,1) | (1,1) | (2,1)
- - - - - - - - - - -
(0,2) | (1,2) | (2,2)
- - - - - - - - - - -
```
